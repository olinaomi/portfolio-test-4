Lets Talk

Brief

CyberSafe Ireland is the Irish children’s charity for online safety. The rationale for the website was to provide simple steps for parents to make changes in the three key areas, Privacy, Screentime and Communication, through
a national awareness campaign.

Solution

For this project we were in groups of two. Myself and my partner, Faye, decided that the most important part of the site should be the education of the parents. We centred our design concept around toys of the 80s, which the parents may have played with. This was to highlight the difference between the nature of toys then and now.

Lets Talk Campaign

The aim of our campaign is to educate parents with facts and statistics about the internet and mobile apps. Our goal was to have a coherent style throughout the micro-site, posters and social media so we used the toys as a recurring theme. As we wanted to educate parents we incorporated the statistics throughout too.
